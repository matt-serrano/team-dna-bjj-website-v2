❓ Do users (customers) need a Stripe account?
No, customers do not need a Stripe account; they simply enter their payment details or use Apple Pay/Google Pay.

❓ Who needs a Stripe account?
Only the admin (gym owner) needs a Stripe account to receive payments and manage payouts.

❓ Can our website act as a controller for payments instead of holding money?
Yes, your site can create and manage payments while Stripe handles processing and sends funds directly to the admin’s account.

❓ Can the admin link their Stripe account through our app?
Yes, using Stripe Connect, the admin can link their account so your app can manage payments on their behalf.

❓ Does connecting Stripe automatically handle everything?
No, Stripe handles payments and subscriptions, but your app must manage users, memberships, and logic.

❓ Where does the money go when a user pays?
The money goes directly to the admin’s Stripe account, not yours.

❓ Do we still need user accounts in our app?
Yes, you need user accounts to track memberships, access, and link users to their payments.

❓ How do we keep our system updated with payments?
Use webhooks so Stripe notifies your app when payments succeed, fail, or change.

❓ What should we use for payments in v1?
Use Stripe Checkout because it is simple, secure, and handles subscriptions out of the box.

❓ Why is this setup important since the site is a gift?
It ensures the business owns its payments and accounts, so you are not responsible for money or long-term maintenance.

FAQ (from original website):
What is BJJ ( Brazillian Jiu Jitsu )
What is BJJ?
Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is believed to have originated in Brazil and is considered to be one of the best forms of martial arts in the world. BJJ is based on the technique that immobilizes the opponent. Which is great if you are smaller in size. It teaches joint locks, chokeholds, and the art to beat an opponent having more physical prowess. BJJ is considered to have the best self-defense techniques in the world. Kids 4-8 do not do submissions until they are older.
Do I need to be in shape to do Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?
No you do not! We all start somewhere, BJJ is a great way to get in shape. We are your cheering squad and you will make progress everyday until we get you to where you want to be! Start a positive habit today.  If you want to come watch a class before you start this is also something we can set up. info@dnabjj.com is a great place to get that going.
Is it good for kids?
Yes. It is great for building confidence. It is a solo sport with a team and a partner. It helps kids who have experienced bullying. During our classes we do anti bullying to help kids handle situations they may face in life.
Is it a good sport for adults?
Yes. It is a great way to learn to defend yourself. It is a fun way to get in shape in a fun way. Bjj is also a wonderful place to make friends and work on healthy goals.
Hi. I am new to bjj. What do I need to know?
How do I get started?
Step One: Fill out our waiver.

Step Two: Fill out the contact page on the program you are interested in. We do not set up intros via the phone. Please always fill out the contact forum. This allows us to contact everyone if we had to switch something due to weather or unseen events. Normally found at the bottom of our website page. www.dnabjj.com

Step Three: We set up a day to do the intro class kids Sunday & adults Monday. I normally reach out a week or two before the date so it is fresh in the mind.

Step Four: After your intro class you can come as much as you like for 7 days following your intro. This allows you to make sure you love bjj. This also allows you to see how much you'd like to come and what works for you or your child. We provide your uniform for your trial. You will have to get your own after the trial. You can get this from us or somewhere else. Please try and get a bjj gi you will just be more comfortable during class.
What will I learn in an intro class?
We will learn how to tie the belt. How to tie our pants so they stay on. How to line up. How to do the warm up. This includes forward rolls, back rolls, how to get up safety, also bjj focused skills to help us in class. Also the intro class allows us to ask questions about bjj, or the hours of classes, or anything on your mind.
Is on day a week good enough?
Absolutely! Brazilian Jiu Jitsu also known as BJJ is all about you and your child. This is something we want our members to do for life. So I always like to ask how many times would you like to do this for the rest of your life. Do not worry if you are new. You can answer this after your first week, month or year. You will be a different person by then. I do not know one person who has ever said I wish I quit. I always here the other saying. I wish I never stopped. We have plans from 1,2,3 and unlimited for your needs.
Will you renew my subscription automatically?
Yes, Our PayPal system will automatically bill you. Please let us know if you would like to pause via email info@dnabjj.com. We want our members happy. This is very important to us.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes. No issues. People move. Things happen. We understand. Please give us 30 days notice. We think this is fair and this allows us time to make sure everything is stopped for you. If you think you will need to pause here and there I suggest you use the PayPal system so you can stop when you need to.
Do you offer any discounts?
We have a family rate with 3+ members paying via the same account. Musty be under the same household.
Can I request a refund?
We do not do refunds. We ask you do monthly at the start and only buy what you will use.
